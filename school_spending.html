Summary: School Spending

This line graph shows the average student performance in math and reading tests. The data is grouped by school spending, with ranges in spending from less than $585.00, $585.00 to $615.00, $615.00 to $645.00, and $645.00 to $675.00. According to this data, the students in schools that spend less than $585.00 per student, or $585.00 - $615.00 per student, have a higher score on math and reading assessments. This data trend continues with the schools that spent $615.00 and up per student having the lowest average test scores.
